LED Keychain

HyTech Racing 2024

Designed the PCB for a flashing LED keychain as part of HyTech Racing's new member onboarding project. The goal was to gain hands-on experience with the full PCB design-to-assembly pipeline: schematic capture, layout, manufacturing, and soldering.

Details
  • Designed the complete PCB schematic and layout in Altium Designer, including component placement, trace routing, and design rule checks (DRC).
  • Submitted the finished design files for PCB manufacturing, then soldered all components onto the physical board, completing the full hardware development cycle from design to assembled product.
